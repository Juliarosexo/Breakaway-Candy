About Fareed Ray

Fareed Ray is a father, author, and CEO of Breakaway Enterprises. His journey has been one of overcoming the misconceptions that shaped his early life, leading to a 21-year prison sentence. As Fareed explains, "His mindset and several misconceptions he chose or grew up to believe caused a lot of his downfalls in life, one of which was a 21-year prison sentence. Then one day, Fareed woke up with a strong desire to right all his wrongs and to help others, especially the youth, change the wrong perceptions we tend to learn all too easily."

Today, Fareed is making a way out of no way by helping others publish books, launching his own clothing line, and creating Breakaway Candy as a symbol of freedom and resilience. The journey is only getting started!

“We are often misled by the misconceptions that society places on us, but those misconceptions are the very things that shape us, test our strength, and lead us to true freedom.”
