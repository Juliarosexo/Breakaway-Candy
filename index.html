About Fareed Ray

Meet Fareed Ray: a relentless entrepreneur, devoted father, and visionary CEO of Breakaway Enterprises. After starting his journey in prison, he discovered his passion for making candy and quickly turned it into a thriving business. With the overwhelming success of his Jailhouse Lollipops, he realized he could break down barriers and take his brand to new heights. Fareed embodies the spirit of "Making a way out of no way," proving that prison walls do not define his future.

“We are often misled by the misconceptions that society places on us, but those misconceptions are the very things that shape us, test our strength, and lead us to true freedom.”
