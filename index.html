Fareed Rays' Story

Fareed Rays is living proof that even in the darkest circumstances, determination and creativity can light the way. While serving a 20-year sentence, Fareed started making candy from scratch, selling it to fellow inmates. His candy became so popular that he earned enough money to start his own clothing line, which allowed him to give back to other incarcerated individuals. An author and entrepreneur, Fareed’s mission is to inspire others to never give up, no matter their situation—showing the world that nothing can stop you if you're determined. Breakaway Candy is a symbol of resilience and hope, proving you can always find a way to make something sweet from a tough situation.
