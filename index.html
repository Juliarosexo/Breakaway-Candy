Fareed Rays' Journey

Fareed Rays, a father, author, and CEO of Breakaway Enterprises, has transformed his life during a 20-year prison sentence by turning a simple candy recipe into a thriving business. What started as a way to bring joy to fellow inmates grew into something much more—a mission to show the world that no matter where you come from, you can create success.

With Breakaway Candy, Fareed embodies the idea of "making a way out of no way," proving that even from the toughest circumstances, something sweet can be born. His journey as an entrepreneur is only getting started, and with each candy sold, Fareed continues to inspire resilience, hope, and change.
