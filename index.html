About Fareed Ray

Fareed Ray is a father, author, and CEO of Breakaway Enterprises. He started making candy from scratch in prison and sold enough to launch his own clothing line, which is now in high demand. Fareed’s journey is a testament to resilience and creativity. Today, he continues to break barriers by sharing his passion for candy, embodying the motto, "Making a way out of no way." He has also helped others publish their books and is dedicated to guiding the youth toward positive paths.

“We are often misled by the misconceptions that society places on us, but those misconceptions are the very things that shape us, test our strength, and lead us to true freedom.”
