Meet Fareed Ray

Fareed Ray: a relentless entrepreneur, devoted father, and visionary CEO of Breakaway Enterprises. Fareed is also an author who has overcome the odds. After starting his journey in prison, he discovered his passion for making candy and quickly turned it into a thriving business. With the overwhelming success of his Jailhouse Lollipops, he was able to sustain his clothing line and is now expanding his candy brand, proving that prison walls do not define his future. Fareed is not stopping anytime soon!
