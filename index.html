About Fareed Ray

Meet Fareed Ray: a relentless entrepreneur, devoted father, author, and visionary CEO of Breakaway Enterprises. With the sales of his Jailhouse Lollipops, he was able to keep his clothing line alive and expand his brand. Fareed embodies the spirit of "Making a way out of no way," proving that prison walls do not define his future.

“We are often misled by the misconceptions that society places on us, but those misconceptions are the very things that shape us, test our strength, and lead us to true freedom.”
