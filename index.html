Meet Fareed Ray

Fareed is a relentless entrepreneur, devoted father, and visionary CEO of Breakaway Enterprises. An author who has overcome the odds, he discovered his passion for making candy during his journey in prison. With the overwhelming success of his Jailhouse Lollipops, he was able to sustain his clothing line and is now expanding his candy brand, proving that prison walls do not define his future.
