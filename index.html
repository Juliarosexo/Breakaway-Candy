Fareed Rays' Journey

Fareed Rays is a father, author, and CEO of Breakaway Enterprises. Serving a 20-year sentence, Fareed transformed his passion into a mission. By making candy in jail and selling it to inmates, he found a way to support his fellow prisoners, inspire them, and ultimately launched his own clothing line. His journey of turning hardships into sweet success exemplifies the belief of "making a way out of no way."

Today, Fareed has published books, launched Breakaway Candy, and is committed to helping others share their stories, just like he has helped fellow inmates publish their works. His journey is far from over, and with every candy sold, every book read, Fareed continues to inspire hope and success.
